SmartChem Explorer

An offline desktop app designed to simulate chemistry experiments such as titration, neutralization, and combustion. It is aligned with the Ugandan New Lower Secondary Curriculum.

Lead-Acid Battery Project

A practical project addressing electricity shortages by building a local model of a lead-acid battery to power school facilities like computer labs and reading areas.

Hydroponic Fodder Production

This innovation project promotes modern, soil-less fodder growing systems for livestock, especially targeting small-scale farmers in Uganda to boost animal nutrition affordably.
